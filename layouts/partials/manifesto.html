Our Mission

Building the
Foundation of Reality

We believe that the next leap in artificial intelligence requires a deep understanding of the physical world.

To build truly general-purpose robotic foundation models, we need data that reflects the complexity, chaos, and physics of reality. Not simulations. Not curated datasets. But raw, egocentric interaction data from the perspective of embodied agents.

MiriLabs is deploying a fleet of data collection systems to capture this information at scale. We are mapping the affordances of the real world to train the brains of tomorrow's robots.

"Intelligence is not just about processing information. It's about embodying it."

Join us in constructing the largest, most diverse dataset of embodied interactions ever created.
